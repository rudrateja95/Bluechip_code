PG Program in Malta

Discover Academic Excellence in Malta

  • University of Malta has been, over its 400-year history, the hub for International academic exchange in the Europe. UM is the leading higher education institution in Malta and its structures are in line with the Bologna Process and the European Higher Education area.
  • We offer UG Foundation Courses, undergraduate bachelor's degrees, postgraduate master's degrees and postgraduate doctorates (PhD). There are Higher National Diploma programs and short courses run on demand in association with ED MALTA. The UG Cert for International Nurses is one of the programs which are in high demand from Indian Sub-Continent.
  • We are a member of the European University Association , the European Access Network, Association of Commonwealth Universities , the Utrecht Network , the Santander Network , the Compostela Group , the European Association for University Lifelong Learning (EUCEN) and the International Student Exchange Programme
  • We're excited to share some great news we've successfully finished the second batch of our PG programs in Malta as of 2024! Right now, we're in the process of wrapping up the 2024 batch, which is set to conclude in September.
  • Our team is here to help you through the admission and visa processes, and we'll assist you in finding part-time employment. After completing your PG in Malta, you'll have the opportunity to work anywhere in Europe by passing the respective country's language test.
